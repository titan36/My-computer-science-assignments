Q: Which of the following are not legal constants in C? Classify each legal constant
   as either integer or floating point.

(a) 010E2
    Could be Octal, but due to the exponent the number must be a legal base10 floating point

(b) 32.1E+5
    Legal floating point

(c) 0790
    An octal value as it starts witha 0, but is illegal because 9 is outside base8 range

(d) 100_000
    Illegal

(e) 3.98e-2
    Legal floating point
