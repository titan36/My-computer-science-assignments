Q: Suppose that i is a variable of type int, f is a variable of type float, and d is a
variable of tpe double. Explain what conversions take place during the execution of the 
following statement: d = i + f;

A: Assignment conversion rules dictate that the expression on the right is converted to
   the type on the left. In this case the result is a double.
