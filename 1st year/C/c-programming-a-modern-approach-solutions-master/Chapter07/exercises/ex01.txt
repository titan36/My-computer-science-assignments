Q: Give the decimal value of each of the following integer constants.

(a) 077
(b) 0x77
(c) 0XABC

A:

(a) 077 is equal to 00111111 in base2, which is 63 in base10.
(b) 0x77 is equal to 01110111 in base2, which is 119 in base10.
(c) 0XABC is equal to 101010111100 in base2, which is 2748 in base 10.
