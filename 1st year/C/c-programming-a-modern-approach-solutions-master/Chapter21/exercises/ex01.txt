Solution to "C Programming: A modern Approach" chapter 21, exercise 1

Locate where header files are kept on your system. Find the nonstandard headers
and determine the purpose of each.

The header files on Debian Stretch are in atleast 2 places. Some are in /usr/include and some are in /usr/lib/gcc. These are all the headers for GCC, that
have man pages.
